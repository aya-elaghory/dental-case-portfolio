Comprehensive Care Case

Management of Lower Right Posterior Pain with Reconstruction of Destructed Posterior Teeth

A young adult male student, medically free, presented with a sudden onset of sharp, throbbing pain localized to the lower right posterior teeth. The pain had been progressively worsening over the past week and was aggravated by cold drinks. He also wanted to improve the esthetics of his teeth.

Patient Young adult male, medically free
Chief Complaint Acute pain in tooth #30
